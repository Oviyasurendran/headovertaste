Veg Frankie


Veg frankie is a popular street food of India, originated from the streets of kolkatta. It is also known by different names like, kati roll, frankie food, frankie recipe or even vegetable frankie recipe. I personally love frankies !! Basically spiced vegetables are wrapped in chapathi or wrap bread to which tomato sauce and green chutney are added to enhance the flavour.


HOW TO MAKE THE DELICIOUS VEG FRANKIE


             
       

  PREP TIME :  10 MINUTES
  COOK TIME :  15 MINUTES
  TOTAL TIME:  25 MINUTES
  SERVINGS   :  2
  COURSE      :  SNACKS
  CUISINE      :  STREET FOOD

INGREDIENTS METHOD 
1 ½ tsp oil
½ capsicum, thinly sliced
2 potatoes, boiled, peeled and mashed
¾ cup paneer/cottage cheese
1 tsp kashmiri chili powder
½ tsp garam masala powder
½ tsp chaat masala
2 tsp butter
3 chapathi or wraps
6 tsp tomato sauce
½ cup lettuce, thinly chopped
1 carrot, thinly chopped
½ onion, thinly sliced
3 tsp green chutney
 
               
1.Firstly,saute capsicum for 2-3 minutes in some oil.
2.Further, add boiled potato and crumbled paneer.
3.Add chili powder, garam masala powder, chaat masala and salt.
4.Mix well and prepare cylindrical shaped sticks. keep aside.
5.Now, add butter onto the tawa.
6.Roast leftover chapathi or wraps until they warm up.
7.Now place the roasted chapathi or wrap on a flat surface.
8.Spread generous amount of tomato sauce.
9.Also place lettuce, carrot and onion in the centre of chapathi.
10.Further, place the prepared cylindrical stuffing of aloo in center.
11.And apply green chutney.
12.Nnow fold the bottom up.
13.Also slowly, fold both the sides one after another.
14.Roll into aluminium foil or butter paper covering the frankie.
15.Finally, veg frankie or kathi roll is ready to serve with tomato sauce.
